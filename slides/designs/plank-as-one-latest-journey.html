PRIVATE FORM · PUBLIC ANONYMOUS PRESENCE09
Automatic resolution

One reservation. Two possible outcomes.

Complete → commit

  • Record completion and progression.
  • Convert the owned reservation into one permanent pixel.
  • Stop the pulse and close the camera.
  • Announce `YOUR PIXEL IS LIVE`.

Fail or leave → release

  • Remove the pending pixel.
  • Return the coordinate to availability.
  • Record no completion or progression.
  • Retry by selecting another available cell.

Alternative routes

Real participation stays distinct from simulation.

HONOR MODE · REAL

Trusted, uninterrupted participation.

  • Reserves a real cell.
  • Shows a real pending pulse.
  • No pause or resume.
  • Leaving releases the cell.
  • Success commits the normal streak and pixel.
GUIDED DEMO · SIMULATED

The complete flow without real effects.

  • Uses isolated simulated pose data.
  • Presence and reservation are visibly simulated.
  • No real streak, progression, or pixel.
  • Never mutates the shared canvas.
  • Always retains the demo label.

Authoritative realtime lifecycle

Presence is a server-owned state machine.

01Select cell

Client requests one coordinate after route, safety, and readiness.

02Reserve atomically

Server enforces one active reservation per participant and coordinate.

03Heartbeat

10-second renewal, 30-second rolling expiry, and an absolute attempt deadline.

04Broadcast lifecycle

Anonymous pending, committed, or released coordinate state only.

05Reconcile

Postgres snapshot is authoritative after reconnect or missed events.

PRIVACY

No public owner ID

No identity, personal timer, form state, camera state, or completion method.

RELIABILITY

Expired means available

Stale reservations cannot block cells indefinitely.

PERFORMANCE

Render on demand

Animate visible pending cells only; degrade the canvas when GPU capacity is limited.
